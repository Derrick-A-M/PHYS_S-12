Week 6: 3D Designing & Printing


This week, my class was assigned to design a component necessary for our final projects, or a small object not easily made by subtractive methods. I chose to complete the former option. My intended final project is to make a wall-painting machine: it would go up and down walls for a certain distance and paint as it moved. In fusion 360, I made what is supposed to be the "body" of my machine. I design the body's frame to have a rectangular shape, so that a paint roller would fit inside or attached to it. i amde the holdster of the roller have an open side, so that the roller could easily slide on or off. I made the pole as an idea for a metal piece, as you cannot 3D print metal. On the top, there is a winch piece that could help the machine move up and down walls.
